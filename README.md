# Fake-News-Detection
A real-time Fake News Detection system using Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing (TF-IDF) that classifies news as REAL or FAKE, helping to reduce misinformation across digital platforms through an interactive Streamlit web application.

🧠📰 **Fake News Detection using Machine Learning**


🚀 **Project Overview**

In today’s digital era, social media platforms are flooded with large amounts of information. Unfortunately, not all of it is true. Fake news spreads faster than real news and can mislead people, influence opinions, and create social panic.

This project aims to automatically detect whether a news article is REAL or FAKE using Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing (NLP).





🌍 **Real-World Problem**

In today’s digital world, information spreads faster than ever before. Social media platforms are filled with both real and fake news, making it difficult for users to verify authenticity.

❗ **Fake news leads to:**

Misinformation & confusion   

Political and social manipulation   

Loss of trust in media   

Rapid viral spread of false content

👉 **Manual** verification is not scalable. So, we need an automated intelligent system.

🎯 **Problem Statement**

With the rapid growth of online news platforms, it has become difficult for users to verify the authenticity of news articles manually. Fake news spreads rapidly and can cause:

Misinformation among people   

Political manipulation   

Public confusion and panic     

Loss of trust in media platforms

👉 **Objective:** Build an intelligent system that can classify news as real or fake based on its content.
